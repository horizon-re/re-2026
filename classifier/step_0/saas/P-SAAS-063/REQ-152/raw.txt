Effective immediately, the schema for Spec v3 will adopt `snake_case` naming conventions for all field identifiers. This update aligns with broader industry practices and improves consistency across internal systems. All new fields added to Spec v3 will follow `snake_case` naming. Existing fields using `camelCase` will remain available for a transitional period but are officially deprecated and will be removed by **2025-10-01**.

To support downstream integration, a **transform** capability has been introduced within the aggregator layer. This module will **dual-accept** both `snake_case` and `camelCase` keys for a period of **90 days** from today. This grace period is intended to give clients and partners sufficient time to migrate their implementations without service disruption.

We strongly encourage teams to begin updating their systems to use `snake_case` exclusively. Updated documentation, including migration guides and revised sample payloads, is now available in the developer portal. These resources include side-by-side examples for ease of transition and common pitfalls to avoid during implementation.

Please direct any technical inquiries or compatibility concerns to the integration support team. Thank you for your cooperation in keeping our schema clean, predictable, and forward-compatible.
